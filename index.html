Brief History

The church property has deep roots in the Middletown area. Historical Methodist records identify the congregation at 1050 Middletown Road as part of the United Brethren tradition in the Allegheny Conference beginning in 1870. Cemetery history connected with the site also notes that a church was built around 1870, with a newer building dedicated later.

Today, Middletown Fellowship Church continues the legacy of Christian worship and community presence in this rural corner of Westmoreland County. This section can be expanded later with photos, church records, anniversary booklets, member memories, and a fuller timeline.
